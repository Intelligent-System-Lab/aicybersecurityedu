Facial Recognition

Facial recognition is a technology that identifies or verifies a person by analyzing patterns based on their facial features.

How Does It Work?

Imagine you are looking at a friend's face. You recognize them because of their eyes, nose, mouth, and the position of these features. Facial recognition works in a similar way. It measures various features of a face and compares them to faces in a database to find a match.

Machine Learning, a branch of Artificial Intelligence, makes facial recognition systems smarter over time. It helps these systems learn from every face they analyze, improving their accuracy.
